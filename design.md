# Industrial Technical Dossier

## Intent

This portfolio is an evidence-led technical dossier for engineering recruiters and operational teams. It should feel precise, credible, and recognisably rooted in mechanical-engineering blueprint language without resembling a generic software portfolio.

## Visual system

- **Genre:** editorial technical dossier.
- **Macrostructure:** Workbench on the home page; Long Document on project detail pages.
- **Tone:** technical, austere, composed, and quietly distinctive.
- **Anchor hue:** muted steel blue, with safety orange reserved for small status and measurement details.
- **Typography:** Bricolage Grotesque for display headings and IBM Plex Sans for reading and interface text.
- **Shape language:** thin rules, clipped technical corners, restrained grid lines, and compact registration marks.
- **Motion:** one orchestrated Hero entrance and one sequential project reveal. Informational sections remain immediately readable.

## Layout rules

- Use a 12-column desktop logic to vary module proportions instead of repeating equal card grids.
- Preserve DOM order and collapse every asymmetric arrangement into a single readable column on small screens.
- Alternate section density and surface treatment to create rhythm without adding decorative content.
- Keep the portrait at its current 4:5 ratio and position. Blueprint details must remain outside the face area.

## Component direction

- **Navigation:** compact technical masthead with a double-rule edge and clear active state.
- **About:** dossier copy paired with a varied fact matrix.
- **Experience:** staggered timeline records.
- **Projects:** asymmetric evidence bento; cards remain complete and independently readable.
- **Skills:** spec-sheet modules with unequal spans.
- **Certificates:** horizontal archive records on wide screens and stacked records on narrow screens.
- **Contact:** technical communication panel with a quieter information rail.
- **Footer:** dense typographic colophon.

## Accessibility and interaction

- Maintain visible keyboard focus and 44px minimum interactive targets.
- Hover treatments apply only to hover-capable devices.
- Reduced-motion users receive static content immediately.
- The blueprint crosshair is decorative, pointer-inert, mouse-only, and hidden over interactive controls.
